Containerized Development with Docker Workshop

During this workshop you will assume the role of Evin, a developer inside a fictitious company called Alpha Office. Over the course of an hour you will follow Evin as he migrates a legacy virtual machine based application into the world of containers. The lab will teach you the fundamentals of Docker administration, development and showcase the resource efficiencies a containerization strategy can provide.
